Background

2021 – present
PhD Candidate
Asian School of the Environment · Nanyang Technological University
My PhD research focuses on investigating disaster risk modelling approaches that account for issues of inequity. Underlying inequalities in society mean that the same hazard can have vastly different impacts on different population groups, and so it matters how risk is measured or modelled. Risk metrics that account for such differential risks better inform policy and resource allocation decisions.
