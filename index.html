Hello, I'm Jeanette 👋

I'm a PhD candidate at the Asian School of the Environment (ASE) and Earth Observatory of Singapore at the Nanyang Technological University. My research challenges conventional approaches of risk analysis and explores alternative risk modelling approaches that account for inequity in disaster risk settings. Prior to my PhD, I was a Research Associate at the Disaster Analytics for Society Lab at ASE, NTU, and have worked in both government and non-governmental organisations.
